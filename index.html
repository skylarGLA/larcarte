Day 1 Lasagna

Original Recipe from

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 jar Classico (tomato & basil) sauce
  • 1 cup tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablesppons white sugar
  • 4 tablesppons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cup(16 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Direction

    Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, oninon, and garlic in a large pan over medium heat unitil well browned.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, salt, Italian seasoning,
    parsely, and black pepper    .
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
    Drain noodles, and rinse in cold water.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350°
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, and 2 tablespoon of parsley, and ½ teaspoon of salt.
  6. In a large baking dish or pan spread 1½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom. Arrange noodles lengthwise
    over the meat sauce. Spread with ½ of the recotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzareela cheese slices.
    Spoon 1½ cups meat suace over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.
    Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevengt sticking,
    either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
    Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
